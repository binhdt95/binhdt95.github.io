About

I am a researcher specializing in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. My research interests include large language models, multi-step reasoning, local differential privacy, and misinformation detection. I have worked on a range of problems spanning knowledge-enhanced NLP, legal AI, and privacy-preserving NLP. Throughout my work, I have participated in various projects and have published my work in several academic conferences and journals. I am always eager to learn, collaborate, and develop innovative ideas to address real-world problems and contribute to the research community.
